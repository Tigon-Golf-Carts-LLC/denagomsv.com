DENAGO® EV ROVER XL6

Specifications for the versatile 6-seater Rover XL6—extra room, extra power, same electric performance.

The 2025 Denago EV Rover XL6 is a 6-seat, all-terrain, street-legal golf cart (LSV) available in multiple colors including Black, Gray, White, Scarlet, Blue, and Verdant. With DOT-approved all-terrain tires and seating for six, the Rover XL6 reaches 25 MPH and starts at $11,995.
