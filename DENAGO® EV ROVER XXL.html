Is the DENAGO Rover XXL Street Legal?

Yes—the DENAGO EV Rover XXL is a street-legal Medium Speed Vehicle (MSV), also classified as a Low-Speed Vehicle (LSV). With a top speed of 25 MPH and seating for 6, it meets the federal LSV definition under FMVSS No. 500, allowing it to be titled, registered, and driven on many public roads posted at 35 MPH or less (rules vary by state and municipality).

To qualify as street legal, the Rover XXL is equipped with the DOT-required safety equipment for LSVs—headlights, tail and brake lights, turn signals, reflectors, mirrors, a windshield, a parking brake, seat belts, and a 17-digit VIN. Learn more about compliance, state requirements, and how every model measures up on our street legal DENAGO MSV golf cart guide.
