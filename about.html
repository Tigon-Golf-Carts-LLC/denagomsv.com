About DENAGO Electric Vehicles

The story behind the DENAGO electric vehicle brand—its mission, its street-legal medium speed vehicle lineup, and where to buy every model from authorized dealer TIGON Golf Carts.

The DENAGO Electric Vehicle Story

DENAGO electric vehicles are a modern lineup of street-legal, lithium-powered vehicles built to reimagine short-distance transportation. The DENAGO EV mission is straightforward: deliver clean, quiet, and dependable electric mobility that replaces the everyday short car trips people take around neighborhoods, resorts, campuses, and job sites. Rather than a recreational golf cart, every DENAGO electric vehicle is engineered as a Medium Speed Vehicle (MSV) / Low-Speed Vehicle (LSV)—road-registrable, DOT-equipped, and ready for real-world use.

The DENAGO electric vehicle lineup spans six models to fit different needs. The DENAGO EV City is the flagship six-seat luxury cruiser with an 80-mile range; the Nomad and extended Nomad XL are versatile four-seat commuters; and the all-terrain Rover series—Rover XL, Rover XL6, and Rover XXL—adds lifted suspension and off-road tires in four- and six-seat configurations. Across the range, DENAGO builds on a common technology platform: a 51.2-volt lithium (LiFePO4/LIPO) battery, an efficient AC motor, a 400-amp controller, and a five-year battery warranty.

What sets DENAGO electric vehicles apart is the combination of genuine street-legal engineering, modern lithium battery technology, and thoughtful features—NFC keyless entry, large touch displays, rear cameras, DOT windshields, and weather-ready comfort. Proudly flying the American flag, DENAGO focuses on EVs that are practical, sustainable, and enjoyable to drive. Every model is backed by an industry-leading five-year battery and two-year parts warranty, is available in a range of colors and configurations, and can be optioned with lift kits, brush guards, cargo racks, and other accessories to match how and where you drive.

TIGON Golf Carts is an authorized DENAGO dealer, and denagomsv.com is the online home for the full DENAGO electric vehicle lineup. We connect you with the right model and handle ordering, financing, nationwide delivery, and post-sale support—so you get the complete DENAGO EV experience from browsing to driveway.
