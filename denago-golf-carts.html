Denago Nomad vs Nomad XL

The Denago EV Nomad and Denago EV Nomad XL are two of the most popular Denago golf carts, and buyers often compare them side by side. Both are 4-seat, street-legal LSVs with a 40-mile range, a 25 MPH top speed, and lithium (LiFePO4) batteries, and both start at $8,995.

The key difference is the frame: the Nomad XL uses an extended platform that adds cargo room and a roomier cabin footprint, while the standard Nomad keeps a more compact profile that’s easier to maneuver and store. If you regularly carry gear or want extra rear space, choose the Nomad XL; if you prioritize a tighter turning footprint, the Nomad is the pick. Compare full specs on the Nomad and Nomad XL pages.
