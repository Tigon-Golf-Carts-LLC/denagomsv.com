DENAGO MSV Quick Start Guide

Every DENAGO MSV shares the same basic operation. When your cart arrives:

  • Charge fully first — plug the on-board charger into a standard outlet and let it complete a full cycle.
  • Power on — use the NFC key card or key switch (varies by model) to enable the cart.
  • Select drive direction — choose forward or reverse, release the parking brake, and drive smoothly with the accelerator.
  • Buckle up — use seat belts for all occupants; your DENAGO MSV is a street-legal LSV.
  • Review local rules — confirm registration and where you can drive before using public roads.

Always follow the detailed startup and safety instructions in your model's owner's manual.

DENAGO MSV Maintenance Schedule

DENAGO lithium carts are low-maintenance, but a little routine care keeps them running their best:

  • After each use: recharge the battery; lithium packs prefer regular topping up.
  • Monthly: check tire pressure, inspect brakes, and confirm all lights and signals work.
  • Every few months: clean battery connections, inspect the charger cable, and check for loose hardware.
  • Annually: have brakes, suspension, and the electrical system inspected by a qualified technician.
  • As needed: keep the cart clean and dry, and store it charged in a sheltered location.

Refer to your owner's manual for the exact service intervals and warranty requirements for your model.
