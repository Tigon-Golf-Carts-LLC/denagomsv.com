Street Legal & Road Ready

Every DENAGO MSV is a factory street-legal Low-Speed Vehicle (LSV) / Medium Speed Vehicle (MSV), equipped with DOT-rated lights, mirrors, a windshield, seat belts, and a registrable VIN. Where state and local law permits, you can title, register, and drive it on public roads posted at 35 MPH or less.

What is the DENAGO MSV golf cart?

The DENAGO MSV golf cart is a line of street-legal electric Medium Speed Vehicles (LSVs) from Denago EV, sold and serviced by authorized dealer TIGON Golf Carts. The lineup includes the City, Nomad, Nomad XL, Rover XL, Rover XL6, and Rover XXL.
