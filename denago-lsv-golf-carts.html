Denago LSV & All-Terrain Golf Carts

TIGON Golf Carts is an authorized Denago EV dealer. Every Denago EV model is a street-legal LSV (Low-Speed Vehicle / Medium Speed Vehicle), and the Denago Rover series adds true all-terrain capability. This guide covers the full Denago LSV lineup and the all-terrain Rover models, with specs, pricing, and dealer support.

What Is a Denago LSV?

A Denago LSV is a street-legal Low-Speed Vehicle designed to operate on approved public roads with a top speed of up to 25 MPH. Denago LSVs are lithium-powered, road-registrable where permitted, and equipped with the safety features required for street use—lights, mirrors, seat belts, and DOT-rated components. Every model in the Denago lineup qualifies as an LSV, making them ideal for neighborhoods, resorts, campuses, and planned communities.

Denago All-Terrain Vehicles: The Rover Series

For buyers searching for Denago all-terrain vehicles, the Denago Rover series is built to handle rougher ground while staying street legal. Rover models pair lifted suspension and DOT-approved all-terrain tires with the same lithium drivetrain found across the Denago lineup.

  • Denago EV Rover XL — 4-seat off-road LSV, 40-mile range, 25 MPH. From $9,995.

  • Denago EV Rover XXL — 6-seat forward-facing all-terrain LSV, 25 MPH. From $11,995.
